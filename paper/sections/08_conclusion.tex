\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We presented HalalChain, an L2 batch traceability protocol for halal certification that combines on-chain immutable audit records with off-chain IPFS document storage.
Contributions include revision history after rejection, Maqasid-aligned design requirement mapping, and an open reproducible implementation.
Qualitative evaluation confirms tamper resistance and role-based access control; quantitative testnet benchmarks are pending Base Sepolia deployment.
Future work will address decentralized auditor governance and mainnet deployment for UMKM producers.
